About

  • Climate Solutions is a London based organisation that's aiming to highlight solutions to resolve the climate emergency.
  • However, you feel about climate change, solutions that replace fossil fuels with renewable energy will lead to a cleaner planet for everybody, and high profile world governmental organisations such as the UK, the EU & China have committed to go zero carbon by 2050 or 2060
  • The aim of this website is to highlight cost effective or low cost solutions, where they exist today or are under development. You do not need to radically alter your lifestyle or pay thousands of pounds.
  • The site will not push political viewpoints
  • Email info@climatesolutions.org.uk, if you would like to assist in writing articles
